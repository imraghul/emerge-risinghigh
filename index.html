✦ The program

3 weeks. 1 transformation.

Each week builds on the last. Three weeks that build on each other. Week 1 – know yourself, understand money, find your voice. Week 2 – validate a real idea. Week 3 – build it with AI and present on Demo Day.

Week 1
Self, Money & Communication
"Know who you are. Understand money. Find your voice."
  • Self discovery – values, identity & emotional intelligence
  • How I think, learn & make decisions
  • Money mindset – saving, compounding & financial basics
Output Life Blueprint +
Finance Plan + Pitch
Week 2
Build & Validate
"From idea to real solution."
  • Systems thinking & problem spotting
  • Idea validation & design thinking
  • User research & feedback loops
  • Structuring your solution
Output Validated Idea +
Solution Blueprint
Week 3
Build & Launch with AI
"Problems everywhere. Builders are rare. Be both."
  • Systems thinking & problem spotting
  • Idea validation & design thinking
  • Build a real app using AI tools – ChatGPT, Claude & more
  • Demo Day – live pitch to judges & parents
  • Speak, write & pitch with clarity
Output Real AI App +
Demo Day Pitch
